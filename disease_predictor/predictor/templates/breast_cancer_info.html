Dictionary

  • Mean Radius: The average distance from the center to points on the perimeter of the tumor.
  • Mean Texture: The average variation in grayscale intensity of the pixels in the image.
  • Mean Perimeter: The average length of the tumor's boundary.
  • Mean Area: The average size of the tumor's area.
  • Mean Smoothness: The average smoothness of the tumor boundary, representing local variations in radius lengths.
  • Mean Compactness: A measure of how compact the shape of the tumor is, calculated as perimeter^2 / area - 1.0.
  • Mean Concavity: The severity of concave portions of the tumor contour.
  • Mean Concave Points: The number of concave portions of the tumor contour.
  • Mean Symmetry: A measure of symmetry of the tumor.
  • Mean Fractal Dimension: A measure of the complexity of the tumor's perimeter.
  • Radius Error: The standard error of the mean of distances from the center to points on the perimeter.
  • Texture Error: The standard error of grayscale intensity variation.
  • Perimeter Error: The standard error of the length of the tumor's boundary.
  • Area Error: The standard error of the size of the tumor's area.
  • Smoothness Error: The standard error of smoothness.
  • Compactness Error: The standard error of compactness.
  • Concavity Error: The standard error of concavity.
  • Concave Points Error: The standard error of the number of concave portions.
  • Symmetry Error: The standard error of symmetry.
  • Fractal Dimension Error: The standard error of fractal dimension.
  • Worst Radius: The maximum radius found among the mean distances from the center to points on the perimeter.
  • Worst Texture: The maximum value of variation in grayscale intensity.
  • Worst Perimeter: The maximum length of the tumor's boundary.
  • Worst Area: The maximum size of the tumor's area.
  • Worst Smoothness: The maximum smoothness of the tumor boundary.
  • Worst Compactness: The maximum compactness of the tumor.
  • Worst Concavity: The maximum severity of concave portions of the tumor.
  • Worst Concave Points: The maximum number of concave portions of the tumor.
  • Worst Symmetry: The maximum symmetry of the tumor.
  • Worst Fractal Dimension: The maximum complexity of the tumor's perimeter.
