Breast Cancer Information

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the cells of the breast. It can occur in both men and women, but it is far more common in women.

Common symptoms include a lump in the breast, changes in breast size or shape, and changes in the skin over the breast.

Early detection and advances in treatment have improved the outlook for people with breast cancer. Treatment may include surgery, radiation, and chemotherapy.
